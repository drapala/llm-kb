# DORA 2025 Report — Key Findings (Landing Page Extract)

## Central thesis: AI as amplifier

> "AI's primary role is as an amplifier, magnifying an organization's existing strengths and weaknesses."

> "The greatest returns on AI investment come not from the tools themselves, but from a strategic focus on the underlying organizational system."

This reframes AI adoption as fundamentally an organizational challenge rather than a technological one.

## Research structure

Collaboration between Google Cloud and partners:
- **Premier Partner:** IT Revolution
- **Research Partners:** GitHub, GitLab, SkillBench, Workhelix

The report is paired with the "DORA AI Capabilities Model" — the report describes state-of-the-art; the capabilities model is prescriptive.

## Companion framing

SkillBench's approach ("decomposes focal work, certifies outputs with internal experts, predicts skill-based outcomes") and Workhelix's focus ("where generative AI drives the greatest ROI", "turning investments into measurable gains") suggest the report quantifies AI's business impact with emphasis on strategic deployment over blanket adoption.
